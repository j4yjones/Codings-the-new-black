About CodeFour

Everything you wanted to know about the most tech-savvy clothing brand.

We believe we can all make a difference

The tech community is growing every day: not only are 20% of global workforce are working in technology, thousands of students are studying specific programs to pursue a career, and millions more who are learning as an interest. Programming is a scientific discipline at a basic level, but is an art form at a more sublime level, it's similar to cooking; a chef will follow a recipe, and each type of dish requires certain ingredients, and the end result is subjective and contains a little bit of your personality.

Our story

We are four women who met from a Front-End Development course with Code First: Girls. We saw potential in each other and believe that if we can code, anyone else can do it. Our mission is to encourage more people getting into the fascinating world of programming through fashion and build a community for all IT professionals and tech enthusiasts. Our products are made of original designs and by programmers.
