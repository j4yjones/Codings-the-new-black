About CodeFour

Everything you wanted to know about the most tech-savvy clothing brand.

We believe we can all make a difference

Our mission is to encourage more people getting into the fascinating world of programming through fashion and build a community for all IT professionals and tech enthusiasts. The tech community is much bigger than we thought: not only are 20% of global workforce are working in technology, thousands of students are studying specific programs to become a "techy", and million more who are learning as an interest.

Our story

We are four women who met from a Front-End Development course with Code First: Girls. We saw potential in each other and believe that if we can code, anyone else can do it.
